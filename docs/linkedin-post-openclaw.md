Orbit Core now helps sales teams too.

We just shipped OpenClaw — a full commercial pipeline source built into Orbit Core. The same observability engine that tracks security alerts and infrastructure metrics now monitors your revenue pipeline with the same precision.

The dashboard gives you 8 real-time KPIs at a glance: MRR, pipeline value, conversion rate, NPS score, open deals, new leads, average deal size, and churn rate. Each card shows trend deltas so you can spot shifts immediately.

Under the hood, it's the same EPS (events per second) engine. Every lead created, proposal sent, contract signed, and deal closed flows through the ingest pipeline as structured events — queryable, alertable, and correlated with the rest of your data.

What's included:
- MRR evolution chart with 30-day trend and glow rendering
- Pipeline funnel with horizontal bar chart — leads to close
- Sparkline trend panels for pipeline value, conversion, leads, and deals
- Stage breakdown bars showing funnel progression
- Real-time activity feed with severity-tagged events
- Two connector templates (events + metrics) ready to plug into any CRM

The entire dashboard auto-refreshes every 60 seconds. It uses the same Chart.js rendering, the same dark neon theme, and the same query API as every other Orbit Core source. No separate tool, no external dependency.

Why build sales tracking into a security/observability platform? Because the teams running IT security in mid-market companies are often the same people managing vendor contracts and service delivery. One platform, one view.

Self-hosted. Your data stays on your server. Always.
